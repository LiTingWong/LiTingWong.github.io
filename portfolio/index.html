Design PhilosophyDesign
Philosophy

"User experience is always my most concern"

From time to time, problems arise in our lives. Engineers then design a solution to serve the needs for the people that encounter the problem. The end destination always goes back to the people. Therefore, I believe the user experience is the greatest factors of any design. Otherwise, if no one is willing to adapt the design, the initial problem is not solved; hence, the design itself becomes a failure to me. However, user-experience is not a factor that can be directed measured; instead, it is a combination of lots of other factors. To deliver the best experience to users, we have to carefully choose the stakeholders and most important DFX(design for X). For example, in one of my project, in which we designed an extension to increase the desk's work area, since our targeted stakeholder are the students moving between classes, the portability become one of the top consideration when designing, and the functionality is reduced as long as it serves the need. In all, my design philosophy is to always have the greatest empathy to the users, then, try to design the product with the best user-experience.

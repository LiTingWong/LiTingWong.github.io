The Rotman Commerce Pride Alliance empowers, engages, and educates LGBTQ+ and ally emerging leaders through leveraging the collective power of our corporate partners, alumni network, and university support system. Through the development and execution of innovative and forward thinking initiatives, we address business opportunities, develop talent, and create inclusive spaces to maximize human potential.
Divercity Case
DiverCity is Rotman Commerce’s largest case competition, and one of Rotman’s premier events of the year. Hosted at KPMG’s downtown offices in the Bay-Adelaide Centre, this case competition offers students an opportunity to solve a LIVE business case related to a local Toronto LGBT organization. This case competition offers the unique opportunity for each competing team to be mentored by an industry professional on the day of the event regarding your case analysis and delivery. Corporate reps will come from a number of firms including KPMG, TD, HSBC, IBM and more.
